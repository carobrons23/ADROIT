OUR SERVICES

Small business setup & support

Adroit Enterprises Incorporated provides business creation support for a myriad of business types. (AEI organizes nonprofit as well as for profit corporations. Bylaws creation, board of directors advisement, business plans, marketing plans, etc).

Branding & Rebranding

Adroit Enterprises Incorporated is a firm that focuses on building brand recognition for client companies. We work to create the image that the client seeks to portray to the public. We build a social media as well as an online presence to create the virtual storefront to introduce the public to the client using search engine optimization strategies. AEI also rehabilitates and rebrands companies that require an updated brand image. AEI utilizes social media, online presence and data analysis to rehabilitate and when required recast corporate brands.

Business Development

Adroit Enterprises provides business support systems for existing business entities. AEI provides business plans, marketing plans, market analysis, financial forecasting / PL projection and all the services you need to ‘Solve Tomorrow’s Problems Today’.

Support Service

QuickenBooks entries and monitoring (Including accounts payable and receivable). We provide 24 hour customer service support.

Adroit Trust Service

A trust is a legal contract that ensures your assets are managed according to your wishes during and after your lifetime. Among the many benefits trusts offer are potential tax benefits and the ability to set parameters for how and when your assets will be used and distributed.
